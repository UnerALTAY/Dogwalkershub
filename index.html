About Us

Dogwalkers' Hub is an online platform aiming at bringing dog lovers together in Berlin. Our goal is to connect dog owners who need help with walking their dogs and people who, for whatever reason, cannot own a dog at the moment but would still like to spend some time with our furry friends. Please have a look at our list of dogs and feel free to contact us to arrange a first meeting! Dog owners can register their dogs using our form at the bottom of the website.

Please note that this is a not a profit-oriented website and we are only willing to be of service to people who would like to walk dogs out of passion.
